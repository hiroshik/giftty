Instructions:

  1. Fill in the name of participants in the setup page.
  2. Once ALL participants are entered. Draw the order of each participant who will pick and unwrap a gift.
  3. Game starts. Ask the first participant to unwrap a gift.
  4. Ask participant if he will keep the gift or steal from others.
  5. If participant chooses to steal, the app will RANDOMLY pick n number of revealed gifts. He MUST select one of them.
  6. The first participant will have a chance to choose to steal or keep the gift at last.
